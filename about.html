WHO WE ARE

Vision Care is an international relief organization dedicated to the prevention of blindness through our global projects and initiatives.

Vision Care is a member of the International Agency for the Prevention of Blindness (IAPB), and participant of ‘VISION 2020’, a global initiative of the IAPB and the World Health Organization (WHO).
We work for the benefit of all people throughout the world who are suffering from vision problems, regardless of their race, color, creed, national origin or religion.

Vision Care has since conducted more than 25 Vision Eye Camps a year and has grown into an international non-profit organization serving 38 countries throughout Asia, Africa and Central-South America.

VISION CARE CORE VALUES

1. Voluntary Service

  • Time, Talent and Treasure
  • Not One-Time Giving But Regular Giving
  • Long-term vision to achieve self sustainable local communities
  • Handover to local doctors after training

2. Professionalism

  • Focusing support based on medical and missions needs
  • Following local customs regulations and culture
  • Working together with local doctors and nurses
  • Strengthening local resources

3. Field-Based

  • Preparation and Research
  • Excellence in Ophthalmology
  • Strengthening Patient care, education, public health, and policymaking

4. Collaboration

  • Doctors and Hospitals
  • Governments and NGOs
  • Missions organizations
